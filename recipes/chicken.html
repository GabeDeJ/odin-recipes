Grilled Chicken

Description

This recipe will leave you with some of the most juicy and delicious grilled chicken you've ever tasted. This recipe will have your guests dying to know it.

Ingredients

  • Raw chicken breasts
  • olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. First mix you chicken breast with all the above ingredients in a bowl until thoroughly mixed.
  2. Light your grill on medium high heat, close lid and let stand for 5 minues. Carefully place seasoned chicken breasts on grill, and cook 5 minutes first side and 3 1/2 minutes on the flip side.take off grill and let rest for 5 minutes. make sure internal temp is atleast 165 Degrees.
