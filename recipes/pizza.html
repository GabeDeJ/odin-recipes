Pizza

Description

This recipe will make you and your familly one of the best pizza pies you'll ever have in your life time, money back guarantee ;)

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Pepperoni (your choice of toppings)
  • Motzerella Cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic Butter

Steps

  1. Roll our your pizza dough and spread out onto an olive oil covered round baking sheet or pan
  2. Spread as much tomatoe sauce over dough as you would like
  3. Sprinkle over cheese and toppings
  4. Plave in oven on 400 for 12 minutes
